About

Garreth Carroll is an Irish portrait artist who investigates and explores the motions of the face and their conceptions to psychological conditions that we as humans face in modern days. Through the medium of paint, illustration and drawing, the portraits of strangers / down and outs and develops a false narrative in the artist mind that is transferred to canvas.

This website has been created to showcase my most recent work. All the images presented on this website have been hand drawn by myself and I reserve the right for all images not to be shared or used without my consent.
